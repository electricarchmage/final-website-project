About

I’m William Johnson — a developer and ML tinkerer who loves turning messy data into elegant tools and neat experiences. I work across the stack: data science, model prototyping, and user-facing interfaces.

Background

My work ranges from building kNN and decision-tree experiments for fraud detection to fine-tuning compact LLMs and crafting desktop UIs in Tkinter. I enjoy bridging the gap between experimental research and product-quality polish.

Skills

  • Python & data science (pandas, scikit-learn, PyTorch)
  • LLM fine-tuning (TinyLlama, Unsloth, Hugging Face)
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, vanilla JS — rapid prototypes & accessible UI
  • Desktop UIs: Tkinter, customtkinter
  • APIs: Discord bots, Tenor/GIF integration

Process

I favor iterative development: quick prototypes, early tests, and then thoughtful refactors. For research projects I emphasize reproducibility and clear evaluation. For UI work I prioritize performance, accessibility, and visual clarity.
