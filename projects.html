Projects

Selection of recent work.

Scholar's Cookbook

The project that represents the sum total of my learning and knowledge gained working through my major and its specification. It is a cookbook-like application that uses a locally-run large language model to give detailed instructions on how to cook any meal. Although it was intended to provide instructions to complete any task, the model ended up being so small that only cooking instructions could be used safely. However, this allowed it to be capable of running on even the lowest-VRAM consumer laptops, which was of great importance to its development environment.

Tech: Python • Tkinter • TinyLlama • JSON

Apocrypha Generator

The semester project for me Artificial Intelligence class. It was a small language model fine-tuned on the entire Bible, allowing it to convincingly generate Biblical apocrypha. Although certain parts of it are depreciated now due to the rapid speed the technology is developing at, it's still up on a Hugging Face Space for examination.

Tech: Hugging Face • GPT-2 • Colab

The Abysswalker's Journey to the Holy City

The final project for a class I took in Fall 2023 titled Animation I, which was supposed to be a sub-five minute animation made in Blender. I found substantial resources including environment and character models from the game Dark Souls and used them for the animation, crafting a wholly original story.

Tech: Blender • 3D Modeling • Content Creation
